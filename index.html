Introduction

Introduction

FlexView is a loyal and friendly React component to work with flex in an easier, standard and more reliable way. Welcome to 2017!

Still, we’re talking about flex… easy, standard and reliable may not be fully true! Therefore, may I present to you ladies and gentlemen the greatest guide for FlexView ever made!(and first one, and only one…probably also the last one)

First of all, why FlexView

This how you flex without FlexView:

// flex
display: flexbox;
display: -webkit-box;
display: -moz-box;
display: -ms-flexbox;
display: -webkit-flex;
display: flex;

// direction
webkit-box-flex-direction: row;
moz-box-flex-direction: row;
ms-flex-direction: row;
webkit-flex-direction: row;
flex-direction: row;

// grow, shrink, basis
webkit-box-flex: 1 1 200px;
moz-box-flex: 1 1 200px;
ms-flex: 1 1 200px;
webkit-flex: 1 1 200px;
flex: 1 1 200px;

And this is how you flex with FlexView:

<FlexView grow shrink basis='200' />

Now that I have your attention we can jump to the real chapters!
